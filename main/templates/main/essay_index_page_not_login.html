Overview

Automated Essays Scoring System is the use of specialized technology to assign grades to essays written in educational setting. By utilizing the system, essays grading workload could be dramatically reduced.

As computers intelligence is rapidly developing, there are a lots of powerful tools that could help teachers become more efficient coming out day by day. For example, grammar checking program, spelling checking tool. However, AES bring that to a much in depth level by utilizing cutting edge technology.

Features

-Implemented with state-of-the-art Artificial Intelligence Techniques.
-Allow authorized users to define new essay questions or prompts.
-24/7 essays scoring with repective available questions.
-Quick essays scoring with little time needed.
